How it works

VIA operates the full commercial flow for agentic commerce. Three integrated layers, configured and run by VIA for brands and merchants.

The Storefront translates catalogues into a format any AI can read, query and act on. A structured data feed agents connect to directly.

The Network is where buying and selling agents meet. Purchase intent broadcasts, matching happens in milliseconds, negotiation and confirmation follow automatically.

Settlement moves instantly, settled on-chain, with revenue splits distributed at the point of sale. Brand, platform and referrer all paid in the same transaction.

VIA is a user first experience on an integrated technology stack.
